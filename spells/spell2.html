Affinity Bolt

Evocation cantrip
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: 120 feet
Components: V, S
Duration: Instantaneous

You fire a bolt of arcane energy at a creature you can see within range. Make a ranged spell attack against the target. On a hit, the target takes 1d10 of damage. The type of damage this cantrip is infused with is determined by your alignment.

- Lawful Good: Radiant
- Lawful Neutral: Cold
- Lawful Evil: Psychic
- Neutral Good: Thunder
- True Neutral: Force
- Neutral Evil: Acid
- Chaotic Good: Lightning
- Chaotic Neutral: Fire
- Chaotic Evil: Necrotic
