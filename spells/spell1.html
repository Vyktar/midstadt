Mystify

Illusion cantrip
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: 60 feet
Components: V
Duration: Instantaneous

You weave a myriad of lies into the mind of a creature you can see within range. The target must make a wisdom saving throw. On a failed save the target takes 1d8 psychic damage.

Your lies can take the form of a harmful creature, vision or effect.
These can include things like;
- Being lit on fire
- Skin melting off your body
- Bones spontaneously breaking
- Being stabbed
- Being bitten or stung by a wild animal
- Being unable to breath, etc.
