Truebronze Mace of Electricity +1

Weapon (Mace), Uncommon (Attunement Required)
This mace is imbued with the power of lightning, creating small bolts of electricity whenever it's bronze meets flesh. Whenever you successful hit a creature with this mace, you deal an extra 1d4 lightning damage. If there is another creature within 5 feet of your target, you may choose to direct all lightning damage towards the adjacent creature instead.
