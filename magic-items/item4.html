Truebronze Glove of Electric Discharge

Wonderus Item, Rare
This glove has the capability of releasing a powerful burst of electricity into any creature being touched by the glove. If the creature in question is unaware of the users presence, they are instantly knocked out for 8 hours if they are zapped by the glove. If the creature in question is aware of the users presence and/or is in combat with the user of the glove, they are instead stunned for six seconds when they are zapped by the glove. The glove has 3 charges that replenish after 24 hours.
