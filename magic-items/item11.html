Nightclaw +1

Weapon (Longsword) [1d8], Rare (Attunement Required)
This pristine longsword was once wielded by Alexander Duskshire, A renowned duke of Gundaria. The blade is made of a rare magic elven metal, with an engraving running up the blade from the hilt that spells out Nightclaw in elvish. Under the light of a full moon, the elvish engraving will glow a pale blue color, and produce a plae blue dim light for 10 ft. When attacking with this longsword, you can trigger a critical strike on a roll of 19 or 20.
