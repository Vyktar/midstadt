Truebronze Mountain Hatchet +1

Weapon (Handaxe), Rare (Attunement Required)
This Hatchet is infused with the energy of a raging snowstorm, causing any creature who has this axe embedded in them to become restrained in a veil of frost. The result is the same regardless of whether the axe was thrown at an enemy from a distance or embedded in a foe at close range. On its turn, the creature can use it’s action to pull the axe out of it’s body, removing the restrained status effect. Additionally, the player wielding the axe can choose to have it fly back into their hands after it was thrown by using their bonus action.
