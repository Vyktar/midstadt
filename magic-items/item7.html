Truebronze Mountain Hatchet +1

Weapon (Handaxe), Rare (Attunement Required)
This Hatchet is infused with the energy of a raging snowstorm. Any creature hit by this weapon must make a constitution saving throw, and if they fail, they will become restrained in a veil of frost. The DC for this saving throw is equal to 10 plus the wielders strength modifier. While restrained, the axe is embedded in the creatures body, essentially disarming the wielder until they decide to remove the axe from the creature in question, or if the creature manages to pull the axe out of there body.
