Truebronze Breastplate +1

Armor (Breastplate), Uncommon (Attunement Required)
This bronze colored breastplate is infused with the power of lightning, creating small bolts of eletricity whenever it is struck by a creature or a weapon. When you take melee damage from a creature while wearing this armor, the creature must make a DC 13 Constituion saving throw. On a failed save, the target takes 1d4 lightning damage. If they succeed, they take no damage.
