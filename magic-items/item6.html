Truebronze Flintlock Rifle +1

Weapon (Firearm, Ammunition, Range (150/600), Two-Handed, Loading) [1d12], Very Rare
This break-action style firearm is a marvel of dwarven engineering, allowing the user to fire a small ball of lead ammunition at high velocity at a target of their choice with high precision. The only trade off for this weapons high damage output is it's slower reload time. After firing a shot from this weapon, the user must spend a bonus action to properly reload the firearm. This weapon cannot be fired again until it is properly reloaded, but can instead be used as a simple bludgeoning melee weapon, dealing 1d8 damage.
