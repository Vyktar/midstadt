Couatl Dagger +1

Weapon, Uncommon (Attunement Required)
You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic dagger.
This dagger has 1d6+3 charges. While holding it, you can use an action to expend 1 daggers charges to cast fly on yourself or another ally within 5 feet of you. When casting it on anyone other then yourself, it requires concentration.
When you first attune to the dagger, roll to determine the number of charges. These charges to not recharge. When you run out of charges or unattune yourself with the dagger before that happens, the dagger loses it's magical properties and reverts back to a mundane ornimental dagger.
