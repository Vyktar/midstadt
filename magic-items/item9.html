Truebronze Greataxe of Beastslaying +1

Weapon (Greataxe) [1d12], Rare (Attunement Required)
This Greataxe strikes fear into the heart of beasts that taste's the bite of it's bronze. Whenever the wielder of this weapon is attacking a beast, the wielder gains advantage with the attack roll. Additionally, beasts that take damage from this weapon have a chance to become gripped with fear. After taking damage, the beast must make a wisdom saving throw with a difficulty check equal to the damage dealt by the attack. On a failed save, the beast becomes frightened. The frightening effect from this weapon can only be applied to beasts that are equal to, smaller then, or one size larger then the wielder of the axe. For example; If a creature of medium size is wielding this axe, that means it can frighten beasts of large, medium, small or tiny size, but not beasts of huge or gargantuan size.
