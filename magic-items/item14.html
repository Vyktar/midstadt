Gthulian Amulet of Refraction

Wonderus Item, Rare (Attunement Required)
This amulet allows the user to at least once per day, ignore the damage of an incoming ranged attack or spell attack, as long at the damage doesn't exceed the wearers total spell save dc or int save dc eequivilant. as a reactions, the user may activate the amulet, upon which the attack or spell that was thrown at them is shattered, and the damage which was supposed to dealt to the player is instead relfected back at the attacker as psychic damage. the opponenet must make an intelligence saving from with a dc of 15 or suffer half of the damage they tried to deal as psychic damage on a failed throw. on a successful save they dont take any damage but the damage they tried to deal to the player is still negated.
