Blood Mail Ring

Wonderous Item, Rare (Attunement Required) (13 Constitution Required)
This ring allows the user the generate a magical suit of armor around themselves as an action. When they do this, the user gains a number of temporary hitpoints equal to there constituion modifier, and overrides the current AC of any armor they are wearing under the generated armor.
This new armor grants 16 AC, does not increase with dexterity modifiers, and grants disadvantage with stealth rolls. When the user dispells the armor, the temporary hitpoints are lost, and cannot be regained in this way again until the user finishes a long rest.
