Necrostaff +1

Weapon, Uncommon (Attunement Required)
You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic quarterstaff. While you hold it, you gain a +1 bonus to spell attack rolls.
This staff has 3 charges. While holding it, you can use an action to expend one of the staff's charges to cast animate dead. The staff regains 1d6/2 expended charges daily at midnight. If you expend the last charge, roll a d20. On a 1, the staff crumbles into dust.
